Virat Kohli's 50th ODI Century

Virat Kohli scored his 50th ODI century against New Zealand who has been India's tough opponent in the Knockout matches.
He is in the sublime form in World Cup 2023. New Zealand has been India's headache for quite sometime now in the ICC tournaments knockout matches but thanks to Virat Kohli this time for his brilliant innings of 117 runs which helped India to secure India's place in the World Cups final match.
